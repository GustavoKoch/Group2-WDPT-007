Old Fashioned

Nr of Servings:

4

2 tsp or 1 tsp

sugar syrup or granulated sugar

1-2 dashes

Angostura bitters

1 splash

water

60 ml

Scotch whisky or bourbon

enough

soda water (optional)

For the rim of the glass:

orange slice, maraschino cherry (optional)

  1. Put the sugar, bitters and water in a small tumbler.Mix until the sugar dissolves if using granulated.

  2. Fill your glass with ice and stir in the whisky. Add a splash of soda water if you like and mix. Garnish with the orange and cherry.
