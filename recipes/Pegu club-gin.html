Pegu Club

Nr of Servings:

4

2 ounces

London dry gin

3/4 ounces

orange curaçao

1/2 ounces

lime juice, freshly squeezed

1 dash

Angostura bitters

1 dash

orange bitters

For the rim of the glass:

brown sugar

  1. Add the gin, orange curaçao, lime juice, Angostura bitters and orange bitters into a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.

  2. Double-strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
