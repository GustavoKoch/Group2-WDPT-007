Watermelon Daiquiri

Nr of Servings:

4

50 ml

white rum

25 ml

watermelon liqueur

10 ml

lime juice

100 g

watermelon

1 wedge to garnish

watermelon

  1. Put a handful of ice in a blender suitable for crushing ice, along with the rum, watermelon liqueur, lime juice and watermelon flesh. Blitz until just smooth.

  2. Fill a hurricane or other tall glass with ice, pour over the daiquiri and garnish with a wedge of watermelon.
