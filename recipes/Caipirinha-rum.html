Caipirinha

Nr of Servings:

4

1/2

Lime

1 and 1/2 to 2 and 1/2 tsp

superfine sugar, to taste

2 ounces

Cachaça

1

Lime wheel for garnish

  1. Cut half of a lime into small wedges.Place the lime and sugar into an old-fashioned glass and muddle well

  2. Top the drink with cachaça and stir well.

  3. Fill the glass with small ice cubes or cracked ice, stir again, and garnish with a lime wheel. Serve and enjoy.
