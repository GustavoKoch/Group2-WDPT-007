Tom Collins

Nr of Servings:

4

2 ounces

London dry gin

1 ounce

lemon juice, freshly squeezed

1/2 ounce

simple syrup

enough

Club soda, to top

For garnish:

1 lemon wheel and maraschino cherry or strawberry

  1. Add the gin, lemon juice and simple syrup to a Collins glass.

  2. Fill with ice, top with club soda and stir.

  3. Garnish with a lemon wheel and maraschino cherry or strawberry (optional).
